================================================================================
PRICING MODEL
================================================================================

✅ Loss Allocation Formula
   loss_to_tranche = max(0, min(total_loss, D × 100) - A × 100)
   where A = attachmentPoint, D = detachmentPoint

✅ Examples Provided
   - Equity tranche (0-3%): Loss allocation from 0% to 3%
   - Mezzanine tranche (3-7%): Loss allocation from 3% to 7%
   - Senior tranche (7-15%): Loss allocation from 7% to 15%

✅ Pricing Components
   - Protection leg: Value of receiving protection
   - Premium leg: Value of paying coupon (negative)
   - PV01: Risky annuity factor
   - Subordination adjustment: Tranche-specific weighting
