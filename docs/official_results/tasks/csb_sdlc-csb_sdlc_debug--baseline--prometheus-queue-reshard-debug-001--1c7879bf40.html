The intermittent stalling of remote-write shards after resharding is caused by a race condition between metrics reset in `shards.start()` and metrics decrement in exiting `runShard` goroutines. **File:** `storage/remote/queue_manager.go`

**Primary Issue Location:** Lines 1237-1266 (`start()` function) and Lines 1563-1578 (`runShard` context cancellation)

**Mechanism:**

When resharding occurs:
1. `reshardLoop()` (line 1189) calls `t.shards.stop()` followed by `t.shards.start(numShards)`
