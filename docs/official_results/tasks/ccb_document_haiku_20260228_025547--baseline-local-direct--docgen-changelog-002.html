### **Pagination API**: Offset-based pagination removed in favor of cursor-based pagination

- **Component**: List operations (flags, segments, rules, namespaces)
- **Impact**: The `offset` field in `ListFlagRequest`, `ListSegmentRequest`, `ListRuleRequest`, and `ListNamespaceRequest` is deprecated

  - Example REST call:
    ```bash
    # Old (offset-based)
    ```
