## Key Classes and Files:

1. **ContentParent** (`dom/ipc/ContentParent.h` and `ContentParent.cpp`)
   - The parent-side actor managing a single content process
   - Handles process creation, IPC communication, and shutdown
   - Implements multiple interfaces for process management

2. **ContentProcessManager** (`dom/ipc/ContentProcessManager.h` and `ContentProcessManager.cpp`)
   - Singleton registry of all active content processes
   - Manages the mapping between ContentParent instances, tabs, and their IDs
   - Provides add/remove/lookup operations for processes

3. **BrowserParent** (`dom/ipc/BrowserParent.h` and `BrowserParent.cpp`)
   - Parent-side tab representation within a content process
   - Maintains reference to its parent ContentParent
   - Handles tab-specific IPC communication

4. **PreallocatedProcessManager** (`dom/ipc/PreallocatedProcessManager.h` and `PreallocatedProcessManager.cpp`)
   - Manages a preallocation pool of ContentParent processes
   - Enables faster process reuse for new tabs and content
   - Respects the `dom.ipc.processPrelaunch.enabled` preference
