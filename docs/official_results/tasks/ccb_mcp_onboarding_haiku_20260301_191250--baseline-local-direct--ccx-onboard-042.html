- **Class**: `NDArrayBackedExtensionArray` (line 92)
- **Purpose**: Wraps a NumPy ndarray as a pandas extension array, storing the ndarray as `self._ndarray`. This enables pandas to layer high-level DataFrame and Series abstractions on top of NumPy's efficient array storage.
